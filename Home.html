Welcome to City Taxi Service

City Taxi (PVT) Ltd is committed to providing a premium and reliable taxi service to passengers across island-wide city areas. Our mission is to ensure that passengers can travel comfortably, safely, and promptly, no matter where they need to go. We focus on providing the best service with competitive pricing and exceptional customer support.

Whether you're a passenger looking for a quick ride or a driver seeking flexible work, City Taxi is here to serve you. Our platform allows passengers to book taxis with ease and offers a smooth, efficient experience for our drivers. Our commitment to quality, safety, and superior customer service has made us the preferred choice in the region.

Our Services

  • Passenger Services - Fast and reliable taxi reservations, SMS notifications, and seamless online payments.
  • Driver Services - Convenient platform to manage bookings, set availability, and earn more with our growing customer base.
  • Customer Care - Dedicated support team to assist with any issues or inquiries, ensuring a smooth ride experience for all.

Why Choose City Taxi?

  • Reliable Rides - Our platform ensures that passengers get to their destination on time, every time.
  • Professional Drivers - Our drivers are fully vetted and trained to provide the best experience.
  • Seamless Payments - Pay easily through our platform at the end of each ride with automated fare calculation.
  • 24/7 Service - Whether it's day or night, City Taxi is always ready to serve your travel needs.
